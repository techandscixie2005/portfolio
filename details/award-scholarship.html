About the Scholarship

The USTC Outstanding Student Scholarship is a prestigious university-level award recognizing exceptional academic performance and holistic development among undergraduate students at the University of Science and Technology of China.

This scholarship is awarded to students who demonstrate excellence not only in academic achievement but also in research, personal development, and contribution to the university community.

Selection Criteria

The USTC Outstanding Student Scholarship considers multiple factors:

  • Academic Performance: Strong GPA and transcript
  • Research Experience: Active participation in research projects
  • Coursework Excellence: Outstanding performance in core subjects
  • Holistic Development: Involvement in extracurricular activities
  • Character and Leadership: Demonstrated integrity and leadership potential
